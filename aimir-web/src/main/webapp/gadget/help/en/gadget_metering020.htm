AIMIR v4.0 Manual

Demand Side Management is the various activities of electric power company to drive customer's reasonable use pattern and meet service needs.

It's also corresponding the concept of Supply Side Management

 

The purpose of Load Management is in order to promote the use of power engineering facilities and reduce load-leveling gaps between maximum load and minimum load. Improvement of the use of power engineering facilities can be achieved by suppressing the peak load demand and increasing midnight demand.

 

The ultimate goal of demand management is for cost reduction and stable electricity demand by managing power demand and improving a load factor.

 

Electricity Demand Side Management (Load Management)

 

Load factor(Load Factor)

 

Load factor is the ratio of average power regarding the maximum power for a period of time. Load factor can be improved by reducing peak power or boosting the ratio of average power. The formula of load factor is as follows.

 

During the terms of reference (average kw / max kw )*100
