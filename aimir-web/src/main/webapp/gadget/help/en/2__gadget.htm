AIMIR v4.0 Manual

Gadget based on a motive of iGoogle has formed of an inactive mode, abbreviated screen (Mini Gadget), the maximum screen (Max Gadget).

Each Gadget contains components of gadget disable, activation, help, refresh, maximize, minimize and delete the gadget.

 

 

①

Minification

Shrinking the Gadget to see only Title Bar. Make it disabled because of no information you see.

②

Help

Showing the help of the gadget.

③

Re-load

Reloading the displayed content in the Gadget.

④

Minimize/Maximize

Switching Mini Gadget <-> Max Gadget.

⑤

Delete

Deleting the gadget on the dashboard.(You can add it again from the gadget setting)

 

 

Mini-Gadget generally shows abbreviated information and summarized graphs. Max Gedget shows detailed information, etc.
