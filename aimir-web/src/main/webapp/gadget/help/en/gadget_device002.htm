AIMIR v4.0 Manual

Providing not only the functions of Modem type, region, the communication state management but also functions of LP log, Event Log, Unit Scanning, Monitoring for a particular modem

 

Mini Gadget

 

Representation of modem type, location, communications status and a number of installed modems in basic query screen.

 

Max Gadget
 

- Lookup and Search

Representation of installed modem list in basic query. In case 24 hours or 48 hours a communication error of meter, it shall be displayed like on the right above. Detail pages are the same as normal.

 

- Detailed Information

You can check the detailed information of modem by selecting the modem you want to check.

Capable to check the information of the modem ID, modem type, SW, HW version, related concentrator and the meter ID.

 

- Schedule

If Zigbee sensor type is like gas, water sensor, The schedule tab shall be activated

 

- History

It's Capable to check a communications log per cycle, change history, alert history, Operation history, battery levels and a history log in History tab.
